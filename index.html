About Me

I am currently a Researcher at Intelligent Creation, ByteDance. I obtained my M.S. degree in computer science from Sun Yat-sen University in 2021, advised by Prof. Guanbin Li and Prof. Liang Lin. Before that, I received the B.E. degree in software engineering from Sun Yat-sen University in 2018.

Research Interests

  • Image Generation & Editing: AIGC, diffusion model, RLHF, image fusion, multi-layer image generation, and controllable visual content creation.
  • Pixel Understanding: Salient object detection/segmentation for video and image, referring image segmentation, open-vocabulary segmentation.
